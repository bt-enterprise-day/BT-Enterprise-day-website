The bank

The bank is an important part of the day. It could determine whether you win or not. You need to adhere to the following rules.

Bank Rules

  • To open your account your company needs to choose a loan amount (be cautious with the amount). You do this at the loan office.
  • Workers work to make money so you must pay them! The figure starts at £225 and is payable hourly.
  • Wages can increase and decrease depending on your success or failure. You may be able to negotiate.
  • The wage increases could depend on inflation and employee satisfaction. Be careful.
  • Interest is charged or added at the end of every hour. If you clear debts late, you may be fried.
  • The bank will close for lunch
  • The Bank Manager will only deal with high ranking company officers for important deals.
  • Transactions will only take place with official company badge wearers.
  • Bank managers are almost always fair and cant be corrupted.
  • Bank reserves the right to alter, defer, create, forget, enforce, and enhance as it sees fit.

The bank should be used wisely. It will earn you some interest, but largely its there for you to pay.
